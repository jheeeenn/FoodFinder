Welcome to Palate Passport!

Explore different kind of cuisines from all around the world

Hi, we are the Faculty of Information and Communication Technology students from UTAR.

We invite you to an adventure to explore the global street foot together. Whether you’re craving the spicy and aromatic flavors of Malaysian street food, the bold and savory dishes of Korea, or the comforting classics of British fare, we’ve got you covered. We also explore the rich and diverse flavors of Mexico, the elegant simplicity of French cooking, the artful techniques of Japanese cuisine, and the aromatic delights of Thailand.

Our mission is to share the unique tastes, traditions, and stories behind each dish, bringing the world’s kitchens into your home. Whether you're a seasoned chef or just beginning your culinary journey, Palate Passport is here to inspire and guide you as you explore new flavors and cultures.
Join us, and let's travel the world through food!
